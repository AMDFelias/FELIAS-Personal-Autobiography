My Childhood

I have lived in a barrio in Kinabjangan since moving from Barangay 3 in 2015. My childhood memories here are full of fun. On weekends, my siblings, neighbor friends, and I used to play Filipino games like tumba lata, tagu-taguan, Chinese garter, and more. It was a lot of fun back then, and that is all.

My Teenage Life

My teenage life started in 2019 when I was a Grade 7 freshman at NNVS. It was a very fun time with many great memories. But in 2020, the pandemic came, and we had to stay at home in quarantine, doing nothing but answering modules. After two years and a month, we finally returned to face-to-face classes. It was so good to see my old classmates and friends again, even with social distancing.

My two years in senior high school changed my life because of all the ups, downs, and challenges I had to face. Meeting new friends from different municipalities and barangays made me feel at home at school and gave me new experiences. When graduation came in April 2025, all my best and worst memories came back to me, and I felt like crying just remembering everything.

Life is full of unexpected changes, but these challenges only make us stronger. Keep moving forward, hold onto your best memories, and never stop believing in your own journey.

My Adulthood

Turning 18 made me realize that it is a new chapter and a new beginning. Starting college and choosing a 'Plan B' program was a big struggle, and I had to face many new challenges. But now, taking Information Technology for one year and two months has made me realize that it is all worth it. I just make sure to never give up on studying, to face college challenges, and to adjust to the new environment on campus. It has been a massive blast full of new experiences, and I will continue doing this until I graduate and achieve my plans for the future.

My Favorites

My Favorite Artist

TWICE is one of my favorite K-pop group because their music is very fun and catchy. Their songs always make me happy and put me in a great mood. The nine members—Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu—are amazing dancers, and they always work very hard together as a team. They look like a real family and have so much fun. Watching them always makes me smile, and that is why I love and support them so much.

My Favorite Food

My absolute favorite food to eat is ice cream because it is sweet, cold, and delicious. My top favorite flavors are chocolate, strawberry, and vanilla. I also really love eating ice cream sundaes with different sweet toppings on top. No matter what the weather is like, having a scoop of ice cream always makes me happy and brightens my day.

My Favorite Place

My favorite place to go to is definitely the sea because I love to relax and listen to the calming sound of the waves. I enjoy walking along the shore, feeling the cool ocean breeze, and watching the beautiful sunset over the water. Being near the sea helps me clear my mind, forget about school stress, and just feel peaceful. It is the perfect place for me to unwind, have fun, and enjoy nature.
